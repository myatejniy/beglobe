Digital Lesson

The project is aimed at early career guidance in the world of the digital economy and the development of digital literacy and computer competencies among schoolchildren. Numbers lessons are available to students from elementary to high school.

"Digital Lessons" project achieved remarkable milestones in just two years. The lessons were accessed 25 million times by children, parents, and teachers globally. We held 300+ open events in 30+ countries, with participation from governors and business leaders.

Media coverage exceeded 2 billion people through 20,000+ publications, earning us prestigious media relations awards like PROBA, RUPOR, and Silver Archer. Our team's dedication and passion made these achievements possible.

VRTech Global

Julia Sabitova built PR, GR, and SMM departments from scratch for PR&marketing support in the EU and the US. Our strategic prowess came to the fore in marketing VR products and orchestrating international events. Notably, we organized the first World of Tanks VR Esports Tournament. The VRTech subsidiary earned the title of the world's fastest-growing VR company, while VRTech Group achieved a groundbreaking feat – hosting the international VR tournament across three countries.

Albus Protocol

CloEE

CloEE is an AI-powered SaaS that enhances the productivity of discrete manufacturing companies of all sizes. CloEE focuses on real-time data collection, leveraging generative AI and predictive analytics to optimize operations and lower costs, reducing emergency stops by up to 95%, improving equipment efficiency by up to 30%, and controlling carbon emissions by limiting energy consumption.

During collaboration with BeGlobe, the company earned the prestigious People's Choice Energy and Innovation Awards, solidifying its position as a pioneer in AI-based manufacturing solutions. In addition, CloEE received the top position in the F6S list of Top 10 Emissions Monitoring Startups and Companies worldwide, further strengthening its position as a prominent player in the industry.
